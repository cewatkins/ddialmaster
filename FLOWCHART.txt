===============================================================================
                            SYSTEM OVERVIEW
===============================================================================

The Divers-Dial Master operates as a continuous loop system:

1. INITIALIZATION: Sets up 8 communication channels, clears memory,
   configures UART, and prepares system tables.

2. MAIN LOOP: Continuously checks for input, processes commands,
   manages channel states, and handles serial I/O.

3. COMMAND PROCESSING: Parses commands in format #<channel><command>
   and executes appropriate channel operations.

4. CHANNEL MANAGEMENT: Maintains state for up to 8 simultaneous
   communication channels with individual buffers and status.

5. SERIAL I/O: Handles UART communication for data transmission
   and reception across all active channels.

The system is designed for continuous operation as a telecommunications
hub, likely for BBS or modem pool applications.
